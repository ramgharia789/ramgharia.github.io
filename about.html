Welcome to Davatt Restaurant, the best place to enjoy authentic Punjabi food and drinks in Batala. We are a family-owned and operated restaurant that serves a variety of dishes from the rich and diverse cuisine of Punjab. Whether you are looking for a hearty meal, a refreshing beverage, or a sweet treat, we have something for everyone.

At Davatt Restaurant, we use fresh and quality ingredients to prepare our food with love and care. Our menu features some of the most popular Punjabi dishes, such as butter chicken, dal makhani, chole bhature, makki di roti, sarson da saag, and more. We also offer a range of Punjabi drinks, such as lassi, chaach, shikanji, and kanji. And don't forget to try our delicious desserts, such as kulfi, gajar ka halwa, jalebi, and rabri.
