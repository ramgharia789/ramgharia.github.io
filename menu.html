Breakfast

One of the most important aspects of Punjabi culture is the breakfast, which is usually a hearty and delicious meal that provides energy and nourishment for the day ahead. Punjabi breakfasts are typically vegetarian. Here are some of the most popular breakfast foods eaten in Punjab:

Parathas

Chole Bhature

Kulcha

Lassi

Lunch

Punjabi lunch consists of a variety of dishes that are rich in flavor, texture, and aroma. Some of the most common ingredients used in Punjabi lunch are wheat, rice, lentils, dairy products, vegetables, and spices. Here are some of the most popular lunch foods eaten in Punjab

Dal Makhani

Paneer Tikka

Makki ki Roti and Sarson ka Saag

Rajma Chawal

Dinner
