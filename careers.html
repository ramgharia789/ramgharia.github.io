Careers

Davatt Restaurant is a popular eatery located in Batala, Punjab. The restaurant is known for its delicious food and excellent service. If you are interested in pursuing a career in the food industry, Davatt Restaurant is a great place to start.

The restaurant offers a variety of job opportunities, including positions in the kitchen, front of house, and management. Some of the available positions include:

*Chef*:


As a chef at Davatt Restaurant, you will be responsible for preparing high-quality dishes that meet the restaurant's standards. You will work closely with other members of the kitchen team to ensure that all dishes are prepared on time and to the customer's satisfaction.

*Server*:


As a server at Davatt Restaurant, you will be responsible for providing excellent customer service to all guests. You will take orders, serve food and drinks, and ensure that all guests have an enjoyable dining experience.

*Manager*:


As a manager at Davatt Restaurant, you will be responsible for overseeing the day-to-day operations of the restaurant. You will manage staff, handle customer complaints, and ensure that the restaurant is running smoothly.

If you are interested in applying for a job at Davatt Restaurant, you can visit their website or contact them directly for more information. To join the DAVATT family, send in your updated resume at
careers@davatt.co.in
