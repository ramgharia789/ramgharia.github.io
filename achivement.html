Some of the achievements of Davatt Restaurant in Punjab are:

  • Davatt Restaurant was awarded the *Best Punjabi Restaurant* by the Punjab Tourism Department in 2022 for its excellence in service, quality, and hygiene.
  • Davatt Restaurant has been featured in several national and international media outlets, such as *The Times of India, **The Hindu, **BBC, and **CNN*, for its authentic and delicious Punjabi cuisine.
  • Davatt Restaurant has a loyal and satisfied customer base of over *10,000* people, who have rated it as *4.5 out of 5* on various online platforms, such as *Google, **TripAdvisor, and **Zomato*.
  • Davatt Restaurant has also participated in several social and environmental initiatives, such as donating food to the needy, supporting local farmers, and reducing food waste and plastic usage.
